Our founding

The Andromeda Aerospace Company is a Portuguese spacecraft manufacturer, launcher, and satellite communications corporation headquartered in Aveiro, Portugal. It was founded in 1995 by Catarina Gomes with the stated goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

Growth & beyond

We achieved what people thought it was a dream. In the 24th of March 2020, the first ever humans to stepped on mars with the help of our technology and cientists, ando wore our badge in their shoulder with pride.
