Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Orders

Unfortunately, we're not able to change an order once an order has been placed. Please check your shipping address, payment methods and merchandise selections carefully. Andromeda Aerospace is not responsible for incorrect addresses or mistakes entered in shipping details.

You can request to cancel your order by contacting support. We will do our best to process these in a timely manner but cannot guarantee customer service will receive your request before the order is in process.

We do our best to ensure the timely delivery of all orders but please bear in mind we are only provided with an estimated delivery date from our carriers. On rare occasions, packages can arrive outside of the estimated timeframe. Andromeda Aerospace is not liable for carrier delays.

Tracking information will be sent to the contact information you provide us. This will be sent via email/sms when your order has been fulfilled. For specific tracking details or if your delivery is late, you can contact the carrier directly for the most up-to-date information, including any carrier or regional delays.

Company

Andromeda Aerospace is a privately owned company.

You cannot invest in Andromeda Aerospace as a retail investor. However, you may consider investing indirectly by buying shares of companies with investments in Andromeda Aerospace, such as Google or Bank of America.

The work culture at Andromeda Aerospace is diverse, inclusive, and equitable. We are a mission-driven company focused on ideas and innovation, and encourage fresh perspectives from bold, diverse voices. At Andromeda, our team is building the future as their unique selves.

To begin with, Andromeda Aerospace has reduced its carbon emissions by switching to renewable energy sources. The company has invested in solar and wind power, and is working to make its entire operations carbon-neutral by the end of 2024.
