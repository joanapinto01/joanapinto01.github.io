Our Fleet

The highly enginnered vehicles that allowed us to push humanity forward!

Falcon 12

The well-known 184 ft high rocket that lifted our astronauts off the ground into the unknown, arriving at Mars in 2022!

Fenix-17

Our very first probe to land in Mars in 2019! It was used to study the Red planet's soil as well as the best places to build an habitat for the future astronauts that would land there.

Nomad Rover

Weighing 2260 pounds, the Nomad Rover was used for transportation along the surface of the Moon and Mars. This vehicle gives the possiblity of exploring without the effects of radiation.

A300 Zero-G

Fully developed in 2010, this airplane is used to do parabolic flights so that zero gravity is achieved in the inside, allowing us to do experiments and train our astronauts to accomodate in this environment.

Zeus-A

In lunar and martian orbit since 2012, this satellite is responsible for analysing/mapping the planets' terrain and helping us plan everything until we got our feet there. It has also been in Earth's orbit since 2011.
