Past Missions

Take a look at some of our past accomplishments

First Astronaut from Andromeda Aerospace to arrive to the moon

We were not the first ones to get there, but we sure made some awesome discoveries that still,to this day, are importante to our lives!

Our collaboration with ISS

In 2010, we started a collaboration with ISS to improve our knowledge of space! This colaboration was very important later when we were in the progress of being the first space agency to get life to Mars!

Our first ever successful probe to arrive in Mars

In 2019, after many tries, Andromeda Aerospace was able to take the very first operationg probe, Fenix-17, to Mars

Falcon-12 brought the very first Humans to Mars

In 2022, Andromeda Aerospace was able to fly humans to Mars during a mission that went along for almost 2 years!
